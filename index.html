About Us

ACES refers to the Association of Computer Engineering Students from the prestigious School of Engineering, Cochin University of Science and technology (CUSAT).

Association of Computer Engineering Students, School of Engineering, CUSAT was found with a vision to across the boundaries of textbooks and explore a whole new world of technologies thus evolving as a complete engineer. It has strength of over 400 young aspirants. ACES organizes various programs such as seminars, webinars, alumni meet, online projects, CUSAT site maintenance, placement training with tech fest being one of its most successful endeavors.
